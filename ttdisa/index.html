Description

Things To Do in San Andreas (TTDISA) is a modification for Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas which extends and expands the game significantly using as much official content as possible.


The game expands on:

  • Storyline: By utilising unused and cut content, the storyline now features new missions, new objectives for existing missions and countless new lines of dialogue. Along with an even larger amount of custom-made small improvements.

  • Side Missions: Various new side activities have been added, carried over from Vice City, Liberty City Stories and Vice City Stories, with one of them tying into the storyline.

  • Vehicles: Various existing vehicles have been expanded upon with new variations and modifications. Cut vehicles, like the FBI Car and the Bloodring Banger B, and new vehicles from other 3D GTA games, like the Kuruma, Stinger, Streetfighter and Sabre Turbo, have been added.

  • Engine Sounds: Multiple unused engine sounds have been restored and given to certain vehicles that previously used the same sounds as other vehicles to give the game some more variety. Some engine sounds from Vice City have also returned.

  • Characters: Multiple new pedestrians have been added, most with unique dialogue. Many utilise unused models, while other models have been improvised based on their unused dialogue. Multiple storyline characters have received alternative outfits based on pre-release media in certain missions, while some characters that didn't have a unique look at all, have been given one. More variety to gangs and law enforcement has also been added.

  • Map: Map features and fixes from other platform versions have been added to the PC map alongside countless custom made fixes and improvements. Several unused interiors have also been implemented, such as safehouses and other miscellaneous interiors.

  • Weapons: A variety of new weapons have been added, such as the M60, Silenced Mac-10, Silenced PSG-1, among others. All these weapons have been added with the idea of bringing something new and unique to the table.

  • And much more, including but not limited to new statistics, clothing, vehicle spawn locations, hobo behaviour, food vendours and rewards for completing things, including 100% completion and 100% turf control.

Volume II is playable in both English and Spanish, as opposed to only English, like in Volume I.


That being said, TTDISA is (still not and will never be) a beta mod. Despite this being made clear almost fifteen years ago, people have been convinced since the absolute beginning of the mod that this is an accurate representation of beta SA.

TTDISA is a mod that, among other things, restores and REUTILISES unused content, meaning that a lot of unused content is used in a way that R* did not originally intent.


The first volume for TTDISA was released by DeeZire in 2009, after being in development for three years.

This second volume has been greenlit by the original creator, DeeZire, making it the latest official version.


My work on TTDISA started after Crspy, the person that patched up DeeZire's Volume I and started work on a Volume II, left without saying a word, leaving TTDISA unfinished.

I started "TTDISA: Overall Improvements" in 2018 as a continuation of Crspy's Fixed Volume I.

On January 7, 2020, work on this new Volume II officially started, replacing TTDISA: OV, after I assembled a team and got approval from DeeZire.
